WAYANAD


This area is famous for its large amount of camping and trekking trails, breathtaking waterfalls, caves, bird watching sites, flora, fauna and an overall plethora of magnificent sights. This area has been a tourist favourite over the years.Wayanad has India's only earth dam, Banasura Sagar Dam, the second largest in Asia. Wayanad is known as the “Ooty of Kerala” due to its breathtaking scenery and lovely weather. Read on to learn some more exciting facts about intriguing places in Wayanad!Wayanad gets its name from Vayal Naadu, or "The land of the paddy fields". It shares much of its history with Karnataka, having been a possession of many of the dynasties that ruled that state, including the Kadambas, the Chalukyas and the Hoysalas.
